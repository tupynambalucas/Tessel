# 🌌 Tessel - Product Vision

## 🎯 The Vision
To create the most fluid and immersive **Hybrid Spatial Social Ecosystem** on the web. Tessel is not just a game, but a spatial communication layer for the modern internet where synchronous 3D presence meets asynchronous social engagement.

## 👤 Target Audience
- **Digital Communities**: Groups looking for a virtual "third place" that combines deep spatial interaction with persistent text-based social features.
- **Social Gamers**: Users seeking a more interactive and spatial way to hang out with friends, integrated with a familiar social media experience.
- **Social Content Curators**: Users who want to capture, share, and discuss high-fidelity 3D moments on a global timeline.
- **Creators**: Users who want to build and monetize their own 3D social spaces and items.

## 💎 Core Values
1.  **Seamless Hybridization**: Zero friction between the asynchronous "Hub" (social network) and the synchronous "Instance" (3D engine).
2.  **Immersive Presence**: High-fidelity spatial audio and WebGPU-powered visuals that set a new standard for web-based interaction.
3.  **Performant Socializing**: Low-latency interaction and scalable social infrastructure.
4.  **Privacy & Safety**: Encrypted voice chat and robust room/social moderation.

## 🛠 Product Pillars
- **Integrated Social Layer**: Friendships persist through feeds and notifications, ensuring engagement even when not in a 3D instance.
- **Persistent Economy**: Items acquired in the Hub (Marketplace) are immediately equippable and visible in the 3D Instances.
- **Media Bridge**: A seamless pipeline for sharing high-quality 3D captures to the social hub timeline.
- **Authoritative Engine**: Fair and synchronized gameplay for all via centralized server-side validation.
- **WebGPU Mastery**: Leveraging the latest web graphics standards for unmatched visual quality across devices.
