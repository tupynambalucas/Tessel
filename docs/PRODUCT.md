# 🌌 Tessel - Product Vision

## 🎯 The Vision
To create the most fluid and immersive 3D spatial social platform on the web, where voice interaction feels as natural as in the physical world. Tessel is not just a game, but a spatial communication layer for the modern internet.

## 👤 Target Audience
- **Social Gamers**: Users looking for a more interactive and spatial way to hang out with friends.
- **Remote Communities**: Groups looking for a virtual "third place" for informal gatherings.
- **Creators**: Users who want to build and share their own 3D social spaces.

## 💎 Core Values
1.  **Immersive Presence**: High-fidelity spatial audio and WebGPU-powered visuals.
2.  **Seamless Access**: No downloads required; runs instantly in the browser or via a lightweight desktop app.
3.  **Performant Socializing**: Low-latency interaction even in crowded rooms.
4.  **Privacy & Safety**: Encrypted voice chat and robust room moderation.

## 🛠 Product Pillars
- **Authoritative Engine**: Fair and synchronized gameplay for all.
- **WebGPU Mastery**: Leveraging the latest web graphics standards for unmatched visual quality.
- **Spatial Audio First**: Voice interaction as the primary mode of engagement.
- **Customizable Identity**: Expressive avatars and customizable rooms.
